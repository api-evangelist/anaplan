Argues that AI does not eliminate staffing challenges in contact centers but changes them, making workforce planning more important as human and AI agents work side by side. It contends successful integration requires modernizing planning infrastructure to handle hybrid human-AI workflows rather than simply automating existing spreadsheet-based processes.
