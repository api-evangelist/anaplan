Discusses how CPG companies can modernize their revenue growth management (RGM) strategy, defined as aligning pricing, promotion, and product strategies with consumer willingness to pay and shopper behavior to deliver profitable growth. It argues traditional CPG planning methods are outdated for volatile markets and require modern tools and cross-functional collaboration.
