Cites research with Gatepoint finding that 68% of organizations want to optimize how they perform workforce planning due to pressures including AI adoption, volatile skill demands, and fluctuating operating costs. It identifies four key drivers: AI's impact on workforce composition, cost pressures, rapidly changing skill requirements, and location strategy.
